How To Join N.H.D.O.


Membership of NHDO shall be open at all times. Any eligible person can become a member subject to his/her fulfilling the eligibility criteria indicated at Para 4 of the General Rules.

Persons eligible to become members may submit their application in the Membership Application Form at NHDO corporate office, New Delhi.

Membership fee (as applicable according to the prevailing NHDO General Rules at that time) will have to be paid which shall be non-refundable for all types of dwelling units. It may even be financed (if required by the beneficiary) subject to fulfilment of the terms and conditions.

Membership will be granted in terms of Para 6 of General Rules, if the applicant meets the eligibility criteria. The applicant will be allotted a Membership Number in order of receipt of the application in NHDO office. This Membership No. must be used in all correspondance with NHDO and while applying for specific housing schemes.
